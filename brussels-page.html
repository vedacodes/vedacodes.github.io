Brussels

Chocolate, Waffles & European Dreams

Discover the charming capital of Belgium and Europe, where medieval architecture meets modern politics. From the stunning Grand Place to the quirky Manneken Pis, Brussels offers world-class art, incredible cuisine, and that special European charm.

My Brussels Adventure

Brussels surprised me at every turn. What I expected to be a purely political city revealed itself as a cultural treasure trove. The Grand Place took my breath away, the chocolate shops tempted me at every corner, and the friendly locals made me feel at home. This city perfectly balances its role as Europe's capital with its Belgian soul.
