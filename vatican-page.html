Must-See Places in Vatican City

⛪

St. Peter's Basilica

One of the largest churches in the world. Climb the dome for panoramic Rome views.

🎨

Sistine Chapel

Michelangelo's ceiling and Last Judgment. Book early access tours to avoid crowds.

🏛️

Vatican Museums

Incredible collection of art and artifacts. Allow full day and book skip-the-line tickets.

🗿

Raphael Rooms

Stunning frescoes by Raphael in papal apartments. Masterpieces of Renaissance art.

🌟

Vatican Gardens

Peaceful gardens covering half of Vatican City. Guided tours available with advance booking.

📿

Papal Audience

Wednesday audiences with the Pope when in residence. Free tickets required in advance.

When & Where to Book Accommodation

📅 Best Time to Book

3-4 months ahead for peak season. Book early access tours and special experiences. Avoid major Catholic holidays for crowds.

📍 Where to Stay

Stay in Rome near Vatican - Prati, Borgo, or Trastevere neighborhoods. Vatican City has no hotels.

💰 Budget Tips

Book online for discounts. Visit on last Sunday morning of month for free museum entry. Bring water and snacks as Vatican food is expensive.
