Must-See Places in Los Angeles

🎬

Hollywood Boulevard

Walk the Walk of Fame and visit TCL Chinese Theatre. Early morning or evening for fewer crowds.

🏖️

Santa Monica Pier

Iconic beach pier with amusement park, restaurants, and stunning Pacific sunsets.

🎨

Getty Center

World-class art museum with stunning architecture and panoramic city views. Free admission.

🌟

Griffith Observatory

Hollywood sign views and planetarium shows. Best visited during sunset for magical lighting.

🏄

Venice Beach

Bohemian boardwalk with street performers, muscle beach, and eclectic local culture.

🛍️

Rodeo Drive

Luxury shopping district in Beverly Hills. Perfect for people-watching and window shopping.

When & Where to Book Accommodation

📅 Best Time to Book

2-3 months ahead for peak season. Book popular restaurants and attractions early. Consider traffic when planning activities.

📍 Where to Stay

Santa Monica: Beach access, trendy restaurants. Hollywood: Central, near attractions. Beverly Hills: Luxury, upscale shopping. Downtown: Arts scene, nightlife.

💰 Budget Tips

Use public transportation and ride-shares. Visit free beaches and hiking trails. Happy hour dining. Stay outside central areas for better rates.
