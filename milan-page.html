Must-See Places in Milan

⛪

Duomo Cathedral

Stunning Gothic cathedral with intricate spires. Climb to the rooftop for panoramic city views.

🛍️

Quadrilatero della Moda

Fashion quadrilateral with luxury boutiques from top Italian and international designers.

🎭

La Scala Opera House

World's most famous opera house. Take a tour or catch a performance if available.

🏰

Sforza Castle

Medieval castle housing museums and Michelangelo's final sculpture, the Rondanini Pietà.

🌳

Navigli District

Historic canal area with restaurants, bars, and nightlife. Perfect for aperitivo culture.

🎨

Brera District

Artistic neighborhood with galleries, boutiques, and charming cobblestone streets.

When & Where to Book Accommodation

📅 Best Time to Book

2-3 months ahead for fashion weeks and peak season. Book La Scala tickets well in advance. Restaurants fill up during Salone del Mobile.

📍 Where to Stay

Centro Storico: Near Duomo, walking distance to attractions. Brera: Artistic area, trendy restaurants. Porta Garibaldi: Modern, business district.

💰 Budget Tips

Visit during August when many locals vacation. Use public transportation. Enjoy aperitivo for light dinner. Shop at outlet stores outside the city.

What to Eat in Milan

🍝

Risotto alla Milanese

Creamy rice dish with saffron, a signature Milan specialty. Rich, flavorful, and comforting.

$12-18
🥩

Cotoletta alla Milanese

Breaded veal cutlet, Milan's answer to schnitzel. Traditional Lombard cuisine.

$18-25
🍷

Aperitivo

Pre-dinner drinks with complimentary snacks. Essential Milan social tradition.

$8-15
🍞

Panettone

Traditional Christmas bread from Milan, now enjoyed year-round. Sweet, fluffy, with candied fruits.

$15-30
